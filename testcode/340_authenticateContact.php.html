authenticateContact

Purpose:

Validates a contact access to the portal: the contact must have an active portal access with correct access dates and give the correct email and password.

Profile:

authenticateContact(email:string,password:string):boolean/ID

Send as:

POST

Parameters:

emailstring primary email of the contact in the application
passwordpassword sent to the contact when he was activated in the application

Response:

false if the contact could not be validated, the contact REST ID if all went well

URL Format:

http://corebos_url/webservice.php?operation=authenticateContact&sessionName=[session id]&email=[email]&password=[password]

Additional Information:
